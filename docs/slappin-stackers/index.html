👏 Slappin Stackers 👏

Two-player local multiplayer competitive action game made in 48 hours with five friends.

For most of us this was our first time making a game in the Godot game engine, and we were interested in how easy it would be to learn. We all agreed by the end that it is much easier to learn than other widely-used engines in 2019 and is a worthy choice for game jams, primarily due to its simplicity and speed.

Download the game here: electric-jammers.itch.io/slappin-stackers
